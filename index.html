About proprietor

I am Bablu Karmakar, the owner of Sapan Engineering Company. With a rich legacy spanning over 25 years, I have dedicated my life to mastering the art of steel fabrication, steel models, and the intricacies of business dealings and management. My journey has been defined by a relentless pursuit of excellence, a commitment to craftsmanship, and a deep understanding of the world of steel. At Sapan Engineering Company, we take pride in transforming raw steel into bespoke creations, and our unwavering dedication to quality is at the heart of everything we do. It is an honor to serve our valued clients and to bring their visions to life through the artistry of steel.

About company

Sapan Engineering Company, led by owner Bablu Karmakar, has been a trusted name in steel fabrication for over 25 years. Our commitment to quality and precision is at the core of everything we do.
